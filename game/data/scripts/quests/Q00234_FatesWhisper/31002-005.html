Maestro Reorin:
I was not satisfied. I had enough Adena that I never needed to work again. I could have retired with my lovely wife to a life of leisure. I was highly respected by all, but still I wanted more. I dreamed of creating a masterpiece that would go down in history. It was all I could think of. It even kept me awake at night! Soon I became as a man possessed, ignoring my apprentices and worst of all my adoring wife. In the midst of this obsession, demons whispered in my ear their words of temptation...
"How did they tempt you?"
